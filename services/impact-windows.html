Why Impact Windows

Benefits of Impact Windows for Your Florida Home

Impact windows are an investment that pays dividends every day ‚Äî not just during hurricane season.

Hurricane & Storm Protection

Impact windows are engineered to withstand flying debris during hurricanes ‚Äî the leading cause of window failure and building damage. The laminated glass may crack on impact but stays intact in the frame, preventing dangerous wind and water intrusion.

  • Tested to withstand large missile impacts (9 lb. 2x4 at 50 ft/sec)
  • Meets Florida Building Code High-Velocity Hurricane Zone requirements
  • Miami-Dade NOA certified for Broward County installation
  • No preparation needed ‚Äî always ready for any storm

Energy Efficiency & Lower Utility Bills

South Florida's heat puts enormous strain on your air conditioning. Impact windows with Low-E coatings and insulated frames significantly reduce heat transfer, keeping your home cooler and your energy bills lower.

  • Reduce cooling costs by 20-30% compared to single-pane windows
  • Low-E glass options reflect solar heat while allowing natural light
  • Insulated frames prevent thermal bridging
  • Energy Star certified options available

Enhanced Home Security

The same laminated glass that stops hurricane debris also makes break-ins extremely difficult. Unlike standard windows that shatter easily, impact glass requires sustained effort to penetrate ‚Äî giving you time and deterring most intruders.

  • Laminated glass is far harder to break through than standard glass
  • Even if cracked, the interlayer holds glass in place
  • Multi-point locking hardware on operable windows
  • 24/7 protection whether you're home or away

Noise Reduction

If you live near a busy road, airport flight path, or have noisy neighbors, you'll immediately notice the difference. Impact windows significantly reduce outside noise, creating a more peaceful living environment.

  • Reduce outside noise by up to 40% compared to standard windows
  • Laminated glass dampens sound transmission
  • Enjoy quieter bedrooms, home offices, and living spaces
  • Particularly effective near airports, highways, and commercial areas

UV Protection

Florida's intense sunlight fades furniture, flooring, artwork, and fabrics over time. Impact windows with UV-blocking interlayers filter out harmful rays while still letting in natural light.

  • Block up to 99% of harmful UV rays
  • Protect furniture, flooring, and artwork from fading
  • Reduce skin damage risk from indoor sun exposure
  • Maintain natural daylight without the damaging effects

Insurance Premium Discounts

Florida insurance companies reward homeowners who invest in hurricane protection. Impact windows can qualify you for significant wind mitigation discounts on your homeowner's insurance.

  • Typical insurance discounts range from 10-45%
  • We provide all documentation for your insurance company
  • Discounts often offset a significant portion of window cost over time
  • Contact your insurer for specific discount amounts
Window Styles

Types of Impact Windows We Install

We install all major impact window styles to match your home's architecture and your functional needs. Every window is custom-manufactured to your exact specifications.

Single-Hung Windows

The most popular style in South Florida. The bottom sash slides up for ventilation while the top remains fixed. Excellent for bedrooms, living rooms, and most applications.

Most Popular

Horizontal Rolling Windows

One or both sashes slide horizontally. Ideal for wide openings and easy operation. Popular for Florida rooms, dens, and areas where vertical space is limited.

Easy Operation

Casement Windows

Hinged on the side and crank outward for maximum ventilation. The entire window opening is unobstructed when open. Great for catching breezes and hard-to-reach areas.

Maximum Ventilation

Awning Windows

Hinged at the top and open outward from the bottom. Can remain open during light rain while still providing ventilation. Often used above doors or in bathrooms.

Rain-Friendly

Fixed Picture Windows

Non-operable windows that maximize views and natural light. Perfect for living rooms, stairwells, and anywhere you want an unobstructed view without ventilation needs.

Maximum View

Custom & Architectural Shapes

Round, arched, triangular, and other specialty shapes to match your home's unique architecture. Custom-manufactured to your exact dimensions and specifications.

Impact windows require minimal maintenance, but a little care extends their life and keeps them operating smoothly:

  • Clean glass regularly with mild soap and water or standard glass cleaner
  • Clean tracks on sliding and single-hung windows to prevent debris buildup
  • Lubricate hardware annually with silicone-based lubricant
  • Inspect weatherstripping yearly and replace if worn
  • Check caulking around frames every few years

We'll walk you through maintenance during your final walkthrough and provide care instructions with your warranty documentation.

Yes, impact windows can crack or break under extreme force, but they're designed to stay intact even when damaged. Here's how they work:

Impact glass consists of two glass panes bonded to a tough plastic interlayer (PVB or SGP). When struck by debris, the outer glass may crack, but the interlayer holds everything together, preventing the breach that allows wind and water into your home.

This is the critical difference from standard glass, which shatters completely on impact. Impact windows are tested to withstand a 9-pound 2x4 lumber traveling at 50 feet per second ‚Äî simulating hurricane-borne debris.
