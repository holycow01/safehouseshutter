Impact Windows vs. Hurricane Shutters

Both protect your property, but they serve different needs. Here's how to decide which is right for you.

Feature Impact Windows Hurricane Shutters
Storm Preparation None required ‚Äî always protected Must deploy before each storm
Daily Benefits Noise reduction, UV protection, energy savings Storm protection only when deployed
Upfront Cost Higher initial investment Lower initial cost
Long-Term Value Adds to home value, daily quality-of-life benefits Protection value; minimal resale impact
Appearance Looks like regular windows Visible when deployed (varies by type)
Best For Primary residences, long-term homeowners Budget-conscious, rental properties, secondary coverage

Pro Tip: Many homeowners combine both ‚Äî impact windows on main living areas and shutters on secondary spaces. We can design a hybrid solution that fits your budget.
